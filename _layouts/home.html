About me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the Networked Systems Group at the University of Edinburgh supervised by Dr. Mahesh K. Marina. I finished the Ph.D. in Telematic Engineering from Universidad Carlos III de Madrid in 2022, under the supervision of Dr. Joerg Widmer.

My research interests are focused on signal and array processing, wireless localization and sensing. More specifically, I am interested in channel decomposition algorithms and off-the-shelf experimentation.
